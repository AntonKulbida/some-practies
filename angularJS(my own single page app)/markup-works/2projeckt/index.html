FOSTERING A GREAT BUSINESS ATMOSPHERE

From home-based cottage industries to major companies on the rise, Encinitas is a great place to do business. We see that as a strength that benefits everyone because economic vitality is the hallmark of a resilient community. We’re now charting new territory, and exploring ways we can become more customer-friendly, foster commerce and help Encinitas reach its full economic potential.

WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW

We’re collaborating with a team of economic leaders in Encinitas, which includes representatives from the Encinitas Chamber of Commerce, the Encinitas 101 Main Street Association, the Cardiff 101 Mainstreet Association, the Leucadia 101 Mainstreet Association, the Olivenhain Town Council, and the San Diego North Economic Development Council. Together with this team, we are studying several focus areas, which include:

  • Innovation
  • Commerce Facilitation
  • Business Mix
  • Visitors & Tourism
  • It’s important that Encinitas remain a city where residents can find jobs and run successful businesses. By forming groups of local businesspeople and citizens to help assess Encinitas’ present and future, we ensure that our community continues to thrive, while maintaining the culture we all love.

For more information and updates about economic development, go to EverythingEncinitas.com.
